About Us

Founded in the year 1996-1997, initially formed by a group of cricket enthusiasts, our club quickly became a hub for local talent. Over the years, it has produced numerous players who have gone on to represent Madhya Pradesh and India at various levels.

The club is known for the best in the category coaching camps, and nurturing young talent, contributing to the development of cricket in Indore. Its facilities have evolved, attracting both amateur and professional players, and it remains a cornerstone of cricket culture in the city.
